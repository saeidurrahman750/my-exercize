The Amazing World of Gumball is an animated sitcom created by Ben Bocquelet for Cartoon Network.[2] Produced by Cartoon Network Development Studio Europe, it first aired on May 3, 2011, and ended on June 24, 2019.[3] The series revolves around the lives of 12-year-old Gumball Watterson, a blue cat, and his goldfish best friend and adoptive brother 10-year-old Darwin, who attend middle school in the fictional city of Elmore, California.
